FAQ

Questions & answers

What is band sync and how does it work?
Band sync lets one person (the "conductor") control the lyrics display for the whole band in real time. When the conductor scrolls, transposes, or changes speed, every connected device follows instantly via Ably's WebSocket infrastructure — sub-100ms latency, fast enough to feel instant on stage.
Does it work without venue WiFi?
Yes. Before the gig, the conductor generates an "offline pack" — a compressed URL of the full setlist. Band members paste it into their browser once and all songs are stored locally. Real-time sync needs internet, but all solo features (scrolling, setlists, tabs) work fully offline.
How many devices can join a session?
There's no hard device limit on Pro. Typical bands of 2–10 members work without issue. Large groups (choirs, orchestras) are welcome — get in touch if you need anything special.
What happens if I cancel my Pro subscription?
Your account reverts to Free at the end of the billing period. All songs and setlists remain intact — you just lose real-time band sync. Resubscribe any time to re-enable it.
Does it work on iPhone and Android?
Yes. BandScroll is a Progressive Web App — it works in any modern browser (Safari, Chrome, Firefox). Add to your home screen for a full-screen, app-like experience. No App Store required.
Is my song data private?
Your songs and setlists are stored in your private Firebase account — only you can access them. We don't read, sell, or share your data. You can delete your entire account and all data at any time from Settings.
